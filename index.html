Vegetable eating benefits

  • Weight loss: Vegetables are low in calories and fat, so they can help you feel full without consuming extra calories.

  • Heart health: Vegetables contain potassium and fiber, which are good for your heart.

  • Digestion: Vegetables contain fiber, which can help with digestion and prevent constipation.
